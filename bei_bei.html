Bei Bei He (荷蓓蓓) at the FlynnSpace

This intimate show marks an East coast debut for Bei Bei He (荷蓓蓓), seeking to extend her reach and begin to build a following across the country. To fans of world music, this show should be a unique experience. The guzheng (Chinese zither) is a complex instrument with many different tones. Expect to hear a choice selection of original compositions.
