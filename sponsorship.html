Endowment

We've recieved a sizable endowment from an anonymous donor who has a strong interest in sparking world music performances in Burlington. This will allow us to kickstart our organization. However, the primary restriction placed on us is that each season, we must have at least four performances of artists who exhibit non-american cultures.
