About Us

The Burlington Vermont String Series is going to be a newly established organization in the Spring of 2018.
We'll be presenting a variety of string music from different cultures. Classical violinists, as well as others who break the mold. Pipa, koto, cello, mandolin, and more.
Organized and put together by a group of string enthusiasts in the Burlington Area.
Seeks to coexist with all currently existing and future organizations in the area.

Our Mission

To provide an engaging and lively experience for the downtown Burlington area:

  1. By sponsoring performances of both well established and up-and-coming string artists
  2. By showcasing a variety of artists from different cultures around the world
  3. By enriching the community through shows accessible to all ages
