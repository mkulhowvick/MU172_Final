About Us

The Burlington Vermont String Series is going to be a newly established organization in the Spring of 2018. We'll be presenting a variety of string music from different cultures. Classical violinists, as well as others who break the mold. Pipa, koto, cello, sitar, and more. Organized and put together by a group of string enthusiasts from around Burlington, we seek to coexist with all currently existing and future organizations in the area.

We'll be showcasing a variety of shows this year, ranging from fiddler Natalie MacMaster, to sitar player Anoushka Shankar, and to close out the season Yo-Yo Ma. All of our shows will take place at a local venue, The Flynn Center for the Performing Arts, on either the MainStage or in the FlynnSpace. Ticketing for shows will become available after the new year.

Our Mission

To provide an engaging and lively experience for the downtown Burlington area:

  1. By sponsoring performances of both well established and up-and-coming string artists
  2. By showcasing a variety of artists from different cultures around the world
  3. By enriching the community through shows with fair pricing that are fully accessible to all ages
